White Water Rafting

The White Water Rafting page was an opening assignment to the Web Fundamentals course at BYUI. This assignment was meant to familiarize and indroduce HTML and CSS elements to students. The White Water Rafting page is a portal page to a theoretical company that offers, as you might suspect, white water rafting tours and equiptment.
